This mod adds a recipe for Calamity item "Charger Test Item" and should be used only as a workaround to bugged "Draedon's Charging Station" and "Draedon's Fuel Factory" in 1.4.5.002 version of Calamity. 
All items needed to craft the item can be found in chests in the Arsenal Labs.
Use at your own risk. 

Recipe:
1x Draedon's Charging Station
1x Draedon's Fuel Factory
10x Dubious Plating
10x Mysterious Circuitry

By Mr. Tavitel
